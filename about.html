About Crue

I'm a pretty simple guy, who takes on every challenge with stride. I aim to please and I work as hard as I can every day to make sure that each task I take on is dealt with to the very best of my abilities. Whether I'm on the archery range, in the office coding, or just having fun with my dogs, my goal is to be the best at what I'm doing, no matter what.
Coming from the construction industry I'm not scared of hard work. I'm always looking for the next challenge and I don't give up on difficult projects. What I don't know, I'll learn. What I'm not good at I'll practice. Humans are resilient beings, there is nothing we can't accomplish together. We are all equal, we all depend on this planet for life, let's work together.
