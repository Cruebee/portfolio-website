About Crue

I'm a pretty simple guy, who takes on every challenge with stride. I aim to please and I work as hard as I can every day to make sure that each task I take on is dealt with to the very best of my abilities. Whether I'm on the archery range, in the office coding, or just having fun with my dogs, my goal is to be the best at what I do.

I've never been afraid of hard work. I spent eight years in the construction industry working sixty hour weeks, pulling over-time, and responding to emergency fixes. When power, water, sewer, or fiberoptic cable was damaged, it was up to us to fix it. It wasn't uncommon to work untill midnight on an emergency call. I remained dedicated to my company untill my body and my soul could no longer support my lifestyle.

While I was working in the fields and on the streets of Colorado, I joined a small group of creative people with a desire to create a video game. I managed to pick up a few things here and ther watching videos and interacting with the team while we coded. However, working sixty hours a week, and trying to find time to code after dinner wasn't producing the quality family time I desired. However, once I got a taste of the coding world, I knew I had to persue an education in development. At the end of 2019, I persued this new goal through CareerFoundry, and forged a new path to make my vision a reality.
